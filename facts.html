Douglas Engelbart

1925-2013

American Engineer, Inventor, Computer and Internet Pioneer

In 20 or 30 years, you'll be able to hold in your hand as much computing knowledge as exists now in the whole city, or even the whole world.
  • Douglas Engelbart's father's name was Carl Louis Engelbart and his mother was Gladys Charlotte Amelia Munson Engelbart
  • He is of German, Sweedish and Norwegian descent
  • His hobbies included hiking, camping, and folk dancing
  • Douglas Engelbart made it his goal to focus his career on making the world a better place
  • He won many awards such as the Yuri Rubinsky Memorial Award, the CHI Lifetime Achievement Award, and the National Medal of Technology (which was awarded to him by the U.S. President, Bill Clinton).
